**Product Requirements Document (PRD): Food Delivery App**

**1. Executive Summary**

The goal of this project is to design and develop a user-friendly food delivery app that connects customers with local restaurants and food establishments. The app will enable users to browse menus, place orders, and track the status of their deliveries in real-time. Our objective is to create a seamless and enjoyable food delivery experience, increasing customer satisfaction and loyalty.

**2. Problem Statement**

The current food delivery market is fragmented, with multiple platforms and websites offering limited options and inconsistent user experiences. Customers face difficulties in finding their preferred restaurants, navigating complex menus, and tracking their orders. Restaurants and food establishments also struggle with managing orders, inventory, and delivery logistics. Our food delivery app aims to address these pain points, providing a streamlined and efficient solution for both customers and restaurants.

**3. Target Users**

* **Primary Users:**
	+ Foodies and individuals seeking convenient meal options
	+ Busy professionals and students with limited time for cooking
	+ People with dietary restrictions or preferences (e.g., vegan, gluten-free)
* **Secondary Users:**
	+ Restaurant owners and managers
	+ Food establishment staff (e.g., chefs, delivery personnel)
* **User Personas:**
	+ Emily, a 28-year-old marketing professional who orders food online 3-4 times a week
	+ David, a 35-year-old restaurant owner who wants to increase his online presence and sales

**4. Functional Requirements**

* User Registration and Profile Management
* Restaurant and Menu Management
* Order Placement and Payment Processing
* Real-time Order Tracking and Updates
* Push Notifications and Alerts
* Search and Filter Functionality
* Reviews and Ratings System
* Integration with Google Maps for Delivery Logistics

**5. Non-Functional Requirements**

* **Performance:**
	+ App launch time: < 2 seconds
	+ Average order processing time: < 30 seconds
* **Security:**
	+ Compliance with PCI-DSS standards for payment processing
	+ Encryption of sensitive user data
* **Usability:**
	+ Intuitive and user-friendly interface
	+ Accessibility features for users with disabilities
* **Scalability:**
	+ Ability to handle 10,000 concurrent users
	+ Easy integration with new restaurants and menus

**6. User Stories**

* As a user, I want to be able to register and log in to the app using my social media accounts or email address.
* As a user, I want to be able to browse and search for restaurants and menus in my area.
* As a user, I want to be able to place an order and pay for it using a secure payment method.
* As a restaurant owner, I want to be able to manage my menu and receive orders through the app.
* As a user, I want to receive real-time updates on the status of my order.

**7. Acceptance Criteria**

* The app launches within 2 seconds of opening.
* Users can successfully register and log in to the app.
* Users can browse and search for restaurants and menus in their area.
* Users can place an order and pay for it using a secure payment method.
* Restaurant owners can manage their menu and receive orders through the app.
* Users receive real-time updates on the status of their order.

**8. MVP Scope**

The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will include the following features:

* User registration and profile management
* Restaurant and menu management
* Order placement and payment processing
* Real-time order tracking and updates
* Push notifications and alerts
* Search and filter functionality

The MVP will be launched with a limited set of restaurants and menus, with plans to expand and add more features in future iterations.

**9. Future Enhancements**

* **Phase 2:**
	+ Integration with wearable devices and smart home systems
	+ Personalized recommendations based on user preferences and order history
	+ Social sharing features to encourage user engagement
* **Phase 3:**
	+ Expansion to new markets and regions
	+ Partnership with food delivery services to increase coverage and efficiency
	+ Development of a web-based platform for restaurants to manage their orders and inventory
* **Phase 4:**
	+ Introduction of new payment methods (e.g., cryptocurrency, mobile payments)
	+ Enhanced security features (e.g., biometric authentication, two-factor authentication)
	+ Integration with popular food review and rating platforms to increase visibility and credibility.
